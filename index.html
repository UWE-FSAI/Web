About UWE AI

Leading Innovation in Autonomous Racing

UWE AI is at the forefront of autonomous racing technology, developing cutting-edge artificial intelligence systems that push the boundaries of what's possible on the track. Our team of dedicated student developers and researchers work tirelessly to create the next generation of intelligent racing vehicles.

Founded with a vision to revolutionise motorsports through technology, we combine advanced machine learning algorithms with precision engineering to deliver unparalleled performance and safety in competitive racing environments.
